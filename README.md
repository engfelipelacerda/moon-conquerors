# Moon Conquerors

Moon Conquerors is a 2D action-survival game developed with Godot.

Humanity has launched a full-scale invasion of the Moon in an attempt to claim its resources and establish control over the lunar surface. As one of the Moon's native alien defenders, your mission is simple: survive, fight back, and protect your homeland at all costs.

Inspired by games such as Seraph's Last Stand, Moon Conquerors focuses on fast-paced combat, enemy waves, progression, and increasingly difficult battles.

## Features

- Fast-paced 2D combat
- Endless waves of human invaders
- Character upgrades and progression
- Unique alien-themed abilities
- Increasing difficulty over time
- Pixel art visuals

## Built With

- Godot Engine
